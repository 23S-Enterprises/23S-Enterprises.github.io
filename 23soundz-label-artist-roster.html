MEET THE GANG

Brodi White

Brodi White is the stage name of Jeremy B Beebe (born August 18th 1991), an American rapper, songwriter and entrepreneur from Ukiah California. While native to Mendocino County, Brodi is currently residing in southern Colorado, where his businesses and recording studio is based. Brodi began his career distributing with UnitedMasters, after a leaving UM in early 2022. Brodi also had a quick stint with Vampire Life before he managed to launch his own music distribution services under his 23S Enterprises holdings company.

Deeze

Deeze was born in Healdsburg CA. June 13th, 1988. Newly signed and distibruted with 23S Enterprises for his long lasting impression on the Mendocino and Sonoma County Hip-Hop scenes. Deeze is the middle child of one older brother and one younger sister. As a child he spent his years relocating from Cloverdale CA. to Ukiah CA./Redwood Valley CA.

Young T.G.

Young TG, Timias Elias Garcia is an upcoming artist from Monte vista Colorado and is the first officially signed artist of 23Soundz. Young and hungry to claim his place in the music industry Young T.G. has been working with Brodi on a weekly basis in the studio to practice and gather what the more experienced artist has to offer. Having donea few practice tracks with releases as singles, TG in on track and preparing new works for release dates TBD. Under that same guidance, TG is set to run up a check on his new EP that is pending release. We've heard samples, and trust this, its HEAT.

MORE YOUNG TG COMING SOON

V.I. Musik

Vicente Phelan, professionally know as V.I. Musik is a very talented rapper, singer and songwriter from Mendocino County. Known for his powerful hooks and smooth rhyme schemes, V.I. makes use of Antares Auto-Tune to produce what has truly become his own signature sound. Primed for a west coast takeover with his consistent work ethic, and extensive catalog of bangers, the young king is well on the verge of doing something great. Coming up in Ukiah California under significantly less than ideal circumstances, VI. Musik brings not only a refreshing sound in the form of radio ready bangers, but an authenticity that cannot be replaced.
